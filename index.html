Newborn wellness, simply explained

Gentle care for your baby’s first days

NeoCare offers calm, practical guidance for first-time mothers so you can feel confident caring for your newborn at home.

Today’s tip

Keep your baby close, warm, and calm during feeding and rest.

🤱

Exclusive Breastfeeding

  • Breastfeed whenever your baby shows hunger cues.
  • Feed on demand for healthy growth and bonding.
🧵

Cord Care

  • Keep the cord dry and clean.
  • Fold diapers below the stump to reduce irritation.
🧸

Keeping Babies Warm

  • Dress baby in one extra layer than you wear.
  • Use a cap and blanket during cool weather.
🚨

Neonatal Danger Signs

  • Seek urgent help for trouble breathing or poor feeding.
  • Call a clinician for fever, lethargy, or persistent vomiting.
💉

Immunization Reminders

  • Keep a simple vaccine calendar at home.
  • Bring your baby to follow-up visits on time.
🧼

Hygiene

  • Wash hands before touching your baby.
  • Keep feeding items and clothing clean and dry.
